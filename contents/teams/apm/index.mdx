## What the APM team does

The APM (Application Performance Management) team builds PostHog's observability products: logs, traces, and metrics.

### Logs

-   Log ingestion
-   Log querying
-   Log storage

### Traces

-   Distributed tracing

### Metrics

-   Metrics ingestion, storage, and querying

## How to work with the APM team

Reach out in our Slack channel [#team-apm](https://posthog.slack.com/messages/team-apm)
